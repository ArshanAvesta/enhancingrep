Understanding Barriers to Clinical Trial Participation

Thank you for participating in this survey. Our goal is to understand the barriers to enrolling diverse patient populations, considering race, ethnicity, gender, and age in catheter ablation clinical trials for AFib patients. Your insights are essential in helping us develop actionable strategies to improve trial enrollment diversity, ensuring that participation accurately reflects the treated population. This survey should take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.

Your responses will be aggregated and anonymized for analysis. The results will not be shared outside of the research team.
